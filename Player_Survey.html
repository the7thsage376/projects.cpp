Resonator-Changli

Changli is a character in Wuthering Waves known for her fiery determination and strategic brilliance.
She serves as the Counselor to the Jinzhou Magistrate and was formerly the Secretary-General in the capital.
Changli is a skilled tactician who excels at leveraging human nature to outmaneuver her enemies.
Her backstory reveals a life shaped by flames, both literally and metaphorically, as she channels her fiery abilities to achieve her ambitions.
Her appearance is striking, with salmon-pink hair, fiery yellow eyes, and burn marks that glow with a fiery intensity.
She is a powerful and versatile character, capable of dealing significant damage in combat.

Resonator-Camellya

Camellya is a captivating character in Wuthering Waves, known as a Bloom Bearer from the Black Shores.
She is a laid-back yet enigmatic figure, exuding an aura of danger and intrigue.
Camellya is deeply fascinated by the concept of fate and follows its guidance as she wanders the lands.
Despite her carefree demeanor, she is a formidable combatant, excelling in germination tests and wielding her Havoc Sword with precision.
Her personality is a mix of hedonism and curiosity, as she seeks out "promising seeds" to challenge and entertain her.
Camellya's unique abilities and mysterious nature make her a standout character in the game's universe.

Resonator-Shorekeeper

Shorekeeper, an imposing and mysterious force within Wuthering Waves, stands as the sentinel of the Black Shores.
As a keeper of secrets and protector of the land, Shorekeeper embodies unwavering resolve and duty.
Cloaked in an aura of quiet authority, they are often seen as a harbinger of change, tethered to the ebb and flow of the waves themselves.
Shorekeeper's mastery over water and tides creates a captivating display of power, symbolizing both fluidity and strength.
Their appearance is an homage to the ocean's majesty, with intricate designs resembling coral and shimmering hues that mimic sunlight reflecting off waves.
Shorekeeper is not just a character; they are a force of nature, a living legend whose actions shape the tides of fate.
